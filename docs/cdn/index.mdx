### Distribution
A **Distribution** is a CDN endpoint that caches and serves your content from Tenbyte‚Äôs global edge network.

- **Origin:** Your source server or storage
- **Domain:** The unique `*.tenbytecdn.com` hostname (or your custom CNAME)
- **Configuration:** Cache rules, headers, and access control policies
